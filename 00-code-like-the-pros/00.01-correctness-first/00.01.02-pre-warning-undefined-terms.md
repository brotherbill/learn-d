# 00.01.02 — Pre‑Warning: Undefined Terms  
You Will See Terms You Don’t Know Yet

The **00‑code‑like‑the‑pros/** series will refer to concepts, tools, and terminology that you may not know yet.  
They will not always be explained immediately.

This is intentional.

Correctness‑first developers learn in layers:

- capture what you understand now  
- skip terms you don’t recognize  
- use search to find meaning when possible  
- keep moving  
- return later with more experience  

Do **not** expect to understand everything on the first pass.  
Expect to understand more on every pass.

As you gain skill, more of this section will become clear.  
Coding discipline grows with exposure, repetition, and experience.

This is normal.  
This is expected.  
This is how professionals learn.

Continue reading, capture what you can, and come back later — the material will make more sense each time.
